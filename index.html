Got questions?

Are there any additional costs associated with the consultation?

Nope, the consultation is on us. The first charge would be for a design of the project.

How long does it take to produce a design?

This depends on the scope of the project. If for small projects, turnaround time could be a couple days. Medium to large projects would be up to two weeks.

When do we discuss the budget?

The budget is discussed after the design of the project has been produced. We will discuss a budget to then come up with a formal proposal.

Can I add more to a project after it is started?

Yes, anything can be added. The con for adding something would be an extension on the timeframe for the project and additional charges for the addition.

What do I do if I do not like the design/price?

If the design is not satisfactory, we can continue to work on the design until it is. The amount of design work would be added to the design fee. If the price is unsatisfactory, we give the option to take it to another local firm to get a price comparison. The design is yours after the design fee is charged.
